These are our News Experiments.

We're interested in how digital media, especially within the browser, allow news to be presented in new ways to improve the reader's ability to understand events within a context.

Index Pages

An index page is the news reader's first impression when visiting the site. There are many ways to structure this page to provide different experiences for the news reader.

  • Highlights
  • Map
  • Pinboard

Annotations

Articles should be able to present contextual information by way of annotations.

  • No annotations
  • Tooltip annotations
  • Popover annotations
  • Margin note annotations

Storyline Pages

Articles about a given story or topic should be published within a storyline to help give them more context, as well as provide a better grouping for the news reader.
